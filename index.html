About Me

I love building tools that help individuals and businesses improve their workflows and outcomes. I am currently a Software Engineer Fellow at Qwasar Silicon Valley where I have built projects in numerous languages and frameworks, with a focus on the fundamentals of software architecture. Previously I was a student at Watch And Code, where I embraced the process of learning software engineering primarily by reading open source codebases. After finishing the core curriculum, I spent about six months as an active contributor to the devtools/debugger of Mozilla Firefox Web Browser. For the first fifteen years of my career I worked as an orchestral musician (French Horn) performing with orchestras across the United States and abroad. When I'm not studying code, I enjoy reading, movies, brewing beer, camping and distance running.

Projects can have many members (with varying permissions), discussion threads (with sub-threads), attachments (text or image files), and tasks (with sub-tasks).
